Project Details

As an introductory project for working with microcontrollers, Sreya Roychowdhury and I designed an automated compost bin that promotes better decomposition. Our bin uses an Arduino UNO, as well as an ultrasonic sensor, servo motor, LED dispay, and DHT11 module to display the current temperature and humidty of the bin, while also self opening and closing when appropriate.
